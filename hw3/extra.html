Extra

In their own words, WURFL.js is JavaScript that detects device models of smartphones, tablets, smart TVs and game consoles accessing your website.
Here are a few of its capabilites:

  • physical_screen_height will retrieve the screen height in millimeters.
  • Similar, though not interchangeable, resolution_height will retrieve the screen height expressed in physical pixels
  • is_smarttv can identify if the device is a SmartTV. Always something important to consider in case a teenage girl has her electronics confiscated.
  • There's also is_robot, which identifies if the accessor is a bot--a "robot, crawler or other programmable agent that stalks the web." Perfect for when the Turing Test fails you!
