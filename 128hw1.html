Resources

Glitch
Glitch is a super convenient way to play around with making webpages and more! You don't even need an account to get started--just pop open one of the many starter templates and get straight to tinkering! They also allow for collaborative coding, and live updates! Definitely a great resource for getting instant feedback.
W3 Schools
The name everyone knows, and for good reason--W3 Schools has incredibly comprehensive guides on nearly everything there is to know about...well, everything? Certainly all of the major coding languages/scripts/etc. I would be remiss not to include them.
StackOverflow
And an honorable mention, since I'm sure most people are using W3 Schools: StackOverflow is where I go when W3 Schools fails me (as rare as it is.) Whenever I'm pondering funky uses or weird issues that I just can't seem to resolve on my own, the good folks who frequent StackExchange usually have you covered. Forums continue to be an infinitely valuable resource in an online world growing increasingly saturated by copycat AI articles.
